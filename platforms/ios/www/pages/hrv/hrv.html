Heart Rate Variability (HRV) Monitor

This task is still under construction. The goals for this task:

  • 1. Measure beat-to-beat heart rate.
  • 2. Track heart rate over time.
  • 3. Calculate variability metrics.
  • 4. Display data in a meaningful way.
  • 5. Data saving/loading functionality.

For those interested, potential metrics include:

  • 1. SDANN: standard deviation of the average beat interval.
  • 2. RMSSD: sqrt of the mean of the squares of interval differences.
  • 3. NN50: # pairs of successive interval differing by > 50ms.
