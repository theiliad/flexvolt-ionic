Initial Setup

  • 1. Turn phone Bluetooth ON
  • 1. Turn computer Bluetooth ON
  • 2. Turn FlexVolt ON
  • 3. In your device's bluetooth settings, find FlexVolt in the list of available devices
  • 4. Pair/connect. code = '0000'

Connecting

After setup, this app should automatically find your FlexVolt device and connect. For connection info, manual connection options, and troubleshooting, tap the connection icon.

Hardware Settings

Once connected, you can change hardware settings, such as sampling frequency, at the configuration page.

Tasks

Home page icons open tasks with different functions.

In a task, press for settings, and for help.
