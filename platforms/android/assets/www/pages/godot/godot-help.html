Go Dot Help
Go Dot moves around the screen based on your muscle activity!

Horizontal (X) = chan 1. Vertical (Y) = chan 2

Motion Modes

Follow RMS tracks the RMS (root-mean-square) of your muscle signal. Stronger signals place the dot further up (Y) or right (X)

Threshold mode moves Go Dot if your muscle signals are above/below set thresholds. Each channel has 2 thresholds. Signals above Top Threshold move Go Dot right/up. Signals below Bottom Threshold move Go Dot left/down. Change thresholds in the settings menu. To experiment, go to the Go Dot Demo.
